    World of Magic

    Me V I B I N G with friends in World of Magic

    An amazing open world game with all sorts of fun adventures just waiting to happen. The combat is decent; the best part about it is the unique spell creation system that allows for countless unique attacks to be created out of a few simple options. The open world, though, is by far the BEST part about it.
    There are NPCs all over the place; hidden in secluded woods, milling around towns, hanging out in randomly placed camps, etc. There are random landmarks all over the place, with chests hidden across the world as well. Even though there is a map, just walking randomly across the game you're sure to have a unique adventure every time. It's even better with friends.
    With a good group of friends, you can have the greatest time of your life. Just chilling at the ocean fishing, decimating hordes of dark wizards for fun, running from the Magic Council (basically cops) after destroying a building, the possibilities are endless. In my words, situations like these "are the definition of V I B E"






    Parkour

    Like World of Magic, it has a cool open world with goodies hidden all over the place. But that's not what makes it fun. The best part about it is the tests of skill.
    Sure, discovering new stuff is fun when you decide to go on a tangent. But if you decide to go on a combo run, that's where I find the game to be the best.
    In the game, you get combo points for doing tricks. You can get various levels of combos by getting enough combo points, going from 1x to 2x to 3x, etc. Thing is, combos have "health." If you stay in one area for too long or go back to it without waiting enough time, then your combo dies. This makes combo running fast paced, constantly searching for new areas to run so your combo doesn't die.
    This means you don't really have time to think on what you're supposed to do to clear an area; you have to just jump in and wing it. It's a really great test of reaction time, hand-eye coordination, and skill.
